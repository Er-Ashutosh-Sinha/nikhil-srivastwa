About Me

          
Crafting Worlds,
One Composite at a Time


          

            I'm Nikhil Srivastwa, a Lead VFX Compositor with over
            12 years of experience delivering high-end visual effects
            for feature films, episodic television, and commercial productions.
            My work spans the full compositing pipeline — from beauty work
              and colour grading to complex CG integration,
              matte painting, and crowd simulations.
          

          

            Beyond the comp, I'm passionate about Python scripting and
            building bespoke pipeline tools in Nuke — Gizmos that
            solve real production problems and free artists to focus on what matters.
            I've been exploring Unreal Engine for real-time VFX,
            virtual production workflows, and AI-driven image generation
            with ComfyUI and Stable Diffusion.
